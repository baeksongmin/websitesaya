Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need special equipment to follow along with the tutorials?

Most of our beginner tutorials are designed to require minimal specialized equipment. At the beginning of each tutorial, our instructors provide a detailed list of materials and tools needed, with suggestions for alternatives when possible. For more advanced tutorials, specific equipment may be necessary, but we always provide information on where these tools can be purchased.

Can I watch the tutorials at my own pace?

Absolutely! Our tutorials are hosted on YouTube, so you can pause, rewind, and revisit them as many times as you need. This makes it easy to learn at your own pace and return to specific techniques that you want to master. The YouTube platform also allows you to adjust playback speed if you want to slow down demonstrations of more complex techniques.

How can I get feedback on my projects?

We encourage students to share their work in the comments section of our YouTube videos. Our instructors regularly check these comments and provide feedback. Additionally, you can join our Facebook community group where both instructors and fellow craft enthusiasts offer support, suggestions, and encouragement. For more personalized feedback, some of our artisans offer one-on-one virtual sessions that can be booked through our website.

Are there tutorials suitable for children?

Yes! We have a selection of tutorials specifically designed for younger crafters (with adult supervision). These are clearly marked with a "Family-Friendly" tag and include simpler projects with age-appropriate techniques. We recommend that an adult reviews the tutorial first to assess suitability based on the child's age, skill level, and the tools involved. Many of our beginner-level tutorials can also be adapted for younger audiences with proper supervision.

Do you provide subtitles or translations for the tutorials?

Yes, all of our tutorials include English subtitles, and many are also available with Malay, Mandarin, and Tamil subtitles. We're continuously working to expand our language options. Since our videos are hosted on YouTube, you can also use YouTube's auto-translation feature for additional language options, though please note that auto-translations may not always be perfectly accurate.
